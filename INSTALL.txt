Install
=======

Copy the folder:
core/packages/vbulletin_by_tools

to:
/YOUR_FORUM_ROOT/core/packages/vbulletin_by_tools

Do not copy api/ai.php to the forum root.

This is a vBulletin 6 package-style starter and follows the same rough layout as packages like googlelogin and wordpresslogin.

After upload, verify:
- /core/packages/vbulletin_by_tools/product.php exists
- /core/packages/vbulletin_by_tools/hooks.php exists
- /core/packages/vbulletin_by_tools/api/ai.php exists
- /core/packages/vbulletin_by_tools/js/tornis_tools_ai.js exists

Then connect/register the product in AdminCP according to your vBulletin install flow.
